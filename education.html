SMA AL-AHMADIAH AL-IJTIMAIAH

Al-Ahmadiah Al-Ijtimaiah Religious High School or SMA Al-Ahmadiah Al-Ijtimaiah for short, is a religious high school located in Pondok Padang Lalang.

    VISSION

  • "Birth people who live islam, knowledgeable and noble character"

    • MISSION

  • Lead, manage and administer the school efficiently and effectively.
  • Applying the Islamic way of life in school more effectively.
  • Concentrating efforts on excellence based on the knowledge of Fardu Ain and Fardu Kifayah.
  • Produce great students in a co-curriculum that is in line with the will of Islam.
  • Creating a safe, conducive and cheerful infrastructure.
  • Using ICT technology optimally.
  • Build good relationships with parents and the local community.

EDUCATION HISTORY

2009-2014

When I was 7 years old, I went to school at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Selasih. This school is located near my house. I am a student who is active in sports. I have represented the school at the district level in netball and volleyball events. Not only that, I also represented my school at the state level in the rhythmic speaking competition. Finally, in the UPSR exam, I got 3A 2B and got sapphire figures.

2015-2019

After graduating from primary school. Next, I continued my studies at Sekolah Menengah Agama Al-Ahmadiah Al-Ijtimaiah in Bukit Mertajam for 5 years. When I was in high school, I was still active in sports. I still represent the school in the netball competition. The highest level I have participated in is the state level. Not only that, I am also a school superintendent. Next, I got 5A out of 11 subjects in PT3 exam and got "jayyid jiddan" in Sijil Menengah Agama (SMA) exam and finally got 3A 8B in SPM exam.

2020-2023

Now, I am continuing my studies at the diploma level in information management at University Teknologi Mara Merbok, Kedah and thank God I got dean from sem 1 to 4 and will finish it in early 2023.
